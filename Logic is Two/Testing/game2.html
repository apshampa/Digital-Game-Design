Logic is Two

A cooperative logic puzzle.

Player 1: SHADOW (W A S D)
Shadow is naturally immune to FIRE. Pushes NOUN blocks.

Player 2: MIST (Arrows)
Mist is naturally immune to WATER. Pushes PROPERTY blocks.

Push blocks next to the bolted IS block horizontally (L-to-R) or vertically (Top-to-Bottom) to create rules!

Game Rules

P1 (Shadow) - W A S D
Immune to Fire.
Pushes NOUNS (SHADOW, MIST, DOOR).

P2 (Mist) - Arrows
Immune to Water.
Pushes PROPERTIES (FLOAT, SAFE, OPEN).

Form logic sentences adjacent to the bolted IS block (Left-to-Right, or Top-to-Bottom).

Press R to Rewind time!
